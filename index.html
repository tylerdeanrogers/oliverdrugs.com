Who TF are you?

This is the Sister City website. Sister City is a hotel with 200 intimate, efficient rooms, created by the studio that made Ace Hotel. It is an experiment in essentialism — a hotel distilled to its most beautiful working parts. It gives you shelter when you need it, but helps deliver you out into the wider world, too. Inspired by Finnish saunas, Japanese bento boxes, the rock-cut cliff dwellings of prehistory and John Cage's 4'33", Sister City considers what’s truly needed for the modern traveler, a list that includes, but is not limited to, the following:

  • A Roof
  • Outlets
  • Plants
  • Soft Feeling
  • Courage
  • Spirit

Every room is a vessel waiting to be filled. There’s a ground-floor restaurant. The address is 225 Bowery, a few doors down from the New Museum. Enter through a garden at the end of an alley. Sister City is on Instagram and Twitter. Email address is hello@sistercitynyc.com. Enjoy.
